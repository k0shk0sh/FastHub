  • (Enhancement) Remove Assignee from Assignees.

  • (Enhancement) Some markdown dark theme enhancements.
  • (Enhancement) Back button in Gist show bring you back to Gists list.
  • (Enhancement) Remove caches of WebView.
  • (Enhancement) Uppercase sentences when commenting from the bottom comment box.

  • (Fix) Lots of bug fixes.
